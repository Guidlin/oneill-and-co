What do we sell?

We supply synthetic oils for compressors handling hydrocarbon gases and mixtures, chemical gases and industrial refrigerants, as well as for vacuum pumps and other equipment.

What kind of oils do we have?

The oils available include Poly alpha olefins (PAO), Polyalkylene glycols (PAG), Polyethylene glycols (PEG), Polyol esters (POE), Hydro treated oils and other special formulations.

We also sell other oils such as: Food Grade Oils, Industrial Compressor Oils and Industrial refrigeration and Air Conditioning Oils.

Learn more about our products.

How much oil do you keep?

We carry stocks of these oils in our UK warehouse in drums (55 US gal, 208 litres) and pails (5 US gal, 19 litres). Larger and smaller containers are available to order.
